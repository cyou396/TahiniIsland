Most people visit Taniti to enjoy the beaches, explore the rainforest, and visit the volcano. There are also many other activities in Merriton Landing, the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay.

  • Beaches
  • Rainforest
  • Volcano
  • Snorkeling
  • Zip-lining in the rainforest
  • Helicopter rides
  • Local history museum
  • Art galleries
  • Pubs
  • Microbrewery
  • Dance club
  • Arcade
  • Bowling
  • Nine-hole golf course (opening next year)
  • Chartered fishing tours
